// The Service

What Is the AI Efficiency Audit?

The AI Efficiency Audit is a structured, 2-hour deep dive into your business operations. Sat comes in — physically or via video — and works through every area of your business methodically: operations, sales, marketing, admin, customer service, finance, and fulfilment.

For each area, we identify what's repetitive, what's rule-based, what takes time but doesn't require human judgment, and what's genuinely complex enough that automation would either fail or be counterproductive. We map the full landscape of what could be automated — and rank it by impact versus effort.

You walk away with a written, prioritised action plan. Not a high-level strategy deck. A concrete document that tells you: here's what to build first, here's what it should cost, here's what you should get back. The kind of document you can hand to an implementer and say "build this."

The Audit is Stage 1 of the 4-stage model — the lowest-risk entry point. If you proceed to implementation (Stage 2), the £495 audit fee is credited against the build cost. If you don't, you've still got a clear, actionable roadmap worth significantly more than the fee.

// Deliverables

What You Get

// Everything delivered in writing within 48 hours of the session.

01

Automation Opportunity Map

A structured breakdown of every area in your business where automation is viable — mapped against your current workflows. Visual and written, covering operations, sales, admin, marketing, CS, and finance.

02

Prioritised Action Plan

Each opportunity ranked by: impact on your business, implementation complexity, and time to value. You'll know exactly what to build first — and why the order matters.

03

ROI Estimates Per Opportunity

For each item in the plan: an honest estimate of what it's worth — time saved per week, cost reduction, revenue uplift potential. Not inflated projections. Grounded estimates based on your actual numbers.

04

Tool and Vendor Recommendations

For each automation, a recommendation on the right tools and vendors. What's appropriate for your business size, your team's technical capability, and your budget. No vendor affiliation — just what's right for the job.

05

What NOT to Build

An honest list of AI use cases that aren't the right fit for your business right now — and why. Worth as much as the positive list. Avoids expensive mistakes before they happen.

06

Stage 2 Scope (if relevant)

If you're interested in moving to implementation, the audit output includes a draft scope for Stage 2 — what would be built, in what order, at what cost. A clear on-ramp to the next stage with no ambiguity.

Who It's For

  • SME with 5–100 employees — enough complexity to automate, small enough to move fast

  • Curious about AI but haven't committed to a specific tool or vendor yet
  • Have a modest budget for implementation if the audit shows clear ROI
  • Want to understand the full picture before spending on build work
  • London-based or UK-based (remote sessions available)

  • Already mid-way through an AI implementation with a specific vendor — this is a pre-build tool

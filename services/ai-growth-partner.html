// The Proposition

What Is the AI Growth Partner?

Most advisory engagements are time-bounded and advisor-paid-regardless. You get the strategy. You get the deck. Then the advisor leaves and the implementation — where value is actually created or lost — is entirely on you.

The AI Growth Partner is a different model entirely. Sat embeds inside your business over a sustained period and takes genuine accountability for outcomes. Not just strategy — implementation, adoption, measurement, and ongoing optimisation. Month by month, getting your business to work smarter with AI.

And the pricing reflects it. Part of the upside is tied to measurable revenue growth. If it doesn't move the needle, Sat doesn't get paid for the part that didn't work. That's what real skin in the game looks like.

This is a fit for owner-managed UK SMEs who are serious about AI transformation — not a chatbot bolted on, not a pilot that never scales — but permanent operational infrastructure that changes how the business runs. Businesses where the owner is ready to commit to the process, not just a conversation.

// The Long Game

Why 3 Years?

// AI isn't a one-time install. It's a living capability that compounds — or quietly degrades without maintenance.

🔄

MODELS UPDATE CONSTANTLY

GPT-4 → GPT-5. Claude 3 → Claude 4. Gemini evolves quarterly. The models your AI runs on today will be replaced by significantly more capable versions within 12–18 months. Active management means you're always on the best available model — not silently running on last year's.

📉

BOTS DEGRADE WITHOUT MAINTENANCE

Prompts drift. Context windows expand and change behaviour. APIs deprecate. A chatbot or automation built in Year 1 and left untouched will silently underperform by Year 2 — and you won't know until a customer notices. Monthly maintenance catches degradation before it becomes a problem.

🛠️

NEW TOOLS EMERGE MONTHLY

The AI tooling landscape in 2026 barely resembles 2024. What's best practice today will be replaced by something dramatically better within 18 months. The businesses pulling ahead are the ones with a partner tracking this and applying it — not reading about it in a newsletter and doing nothing.

🔍

GOOGLE AI SEARCH REWARDS CONSISTENCY

AI-generated search answers pull from sites with structured data, schema markup, and continuously updated content. A static site from 2022 won't appear in AI Overviews. The sites winning in AI search are being actively optimised every single month — not just once at launch.

📈

COMPOUNDING RESULTS OVER TIME

Every optimisation builds on the last. Month 6 is better than Month 1 because you have 6 months of performance data to work from. Month 24 is dramatically better than Month 6. AI transformation compounds — but only if someone is actively managing it, not leaving it to drift.

💰

THE MATHS OVER 3 YEARS

Audit (£495) + Implementation (£3.5–8k) + 36-month retainer (£18–29k) + optional website/SEO add-on (£10.8–18k) = £35,000–50,000 total value. That's the real number. Long-term AI partnership, not a one-off transaction.

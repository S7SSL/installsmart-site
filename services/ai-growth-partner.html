// The Proposition

What Is the AI Growth Partner?

Most advisory engagements are time-bounded and advisor-paid-regardless. You get the strategy. You get the deck. Then the advisor leaves and the implementation — where value is actually created or lost — is entirely on you.

The AI Growth Partner is a different model entirely. Sat embeds inside your business over a sustained period and takes genuine accountability for outcomes. Not just strategy — implementation, adoption, measurement, and ongoing optimisation. Month by month, getting your business to work smarter with AI.

And the pricing reflects it. Part of the upside is tied to measurable revenue growth. If it doesn't move the needle, Sat doesn't get paid for the part that didn't work. That's what real skin in the game looks like.

This is a fit for owner-managed UK SMEs who are serious about AI transformation — not a chatbot bolted on, not a pilot that never scales — but permanent operational infrastructure that changes how the business runs. Businesses where the owner is ready to commit to the process, not just a conversation.

// The Method

How It Works — Three Phases

// A structured, time-bound engagement with clear milestones at every stage.

Phase 1

AI Audit & Strategy

📅 Month 1

Before anything is built, we need to understand the business properly. Sat conducts a thorough AI audit — mapping every operational area against AI opportunity, identifying quick wins versus strategic plays, and building a clear roadmap with measurable targets.

  • Full business and operations audit
  • AI opportunity mapping across all functions
  • Revenue uplift potential identified and quantified
  • Prioritised implementation roadmap
  • Baseline metrics established for performance measurement
  • Agreed KPIs for upside calculation
Phase 2

Implementation

📅 Months 2–3

Strategy without execution is just a document. Sat oversees and drives the implementation of the agreed AI systems — the right tools, the right vendors, the right workflows. Not just recommending, but making sure it actually gets built and adopted.

  • Tool and vendor selection and onboarding
  • Workflow design and automation setup
  • Team training and adoption management
  • Integration with existing systems
  • Build oversight and quality review
  • Early performance tracking against baseline
Phase 3

Ongoing Optimisation

📅 Month 4+

This is where the performance upside kicks in. Once the infrastructure is live, the focus shifts to measuring, optimising, and scaling. Monthly reviews, performance tracking, and ongoing iteration — tied directly to the growth in your revenue.

  • Monthly performance reviews against agreed KPIs
  • Revenue uplift measurement and reporting
  • Continuous optimisation of live systems
  • New opportunity identification as the business evolves
  • Performance-based fee calculation on measurable uplift
  • Quarterly strategic reviews

⚡ What "skin in the game" actually means

The performance component is calculated on measurable, agreed revenue uplift — not projections, not vague "efficiency gains." We establish the baseline in Month 1. We agree the KPIs. The upside is calculated on what actually changes in the numbers. If it doesn't move, that part of the fee doesn't apply. That's what makes this different from every other advisory model in this space.

How is "measurable revenue uplift" calculated?
We establish a baseline in Month 1 — average revenue over the preceding 3–6 months. Uplift is the verified increase above that baseline, adjusted for market factors where applicable. The methodology is agreed upfront and transparently tracked.

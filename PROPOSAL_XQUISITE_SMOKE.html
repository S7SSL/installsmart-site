Executive Summary

Xquisite Smoke has a strong product catalogue, but your technical foundation is holding you back. Our full audit identified critical gaps in SEO, mobile usability, and conversion optimization that are directly suppressing your search rankings, mobile sales, and customer trust. This proposal addresses the most urgent issues—technical SEO and mobile responsiveness—which alone will unlock significant improvements in visibility and conversion rates.

Key Findings

SEO

Your site has several critical SEO gaps suppressing visibility on Google:

  • Missing heading structure — Confuses search engines about page hierarchy
  • Image optimization issues — No alt text, uncompressed files, poor naming
  • Outdated metadata — Inaccurate titles and descriptions across pages
  • Missing structured data — No product schema for e-commerce
  • Weak homepage content — Insufficient text for Google to rank the page

Mobile Usability

The site does not render well on mobile. Navigation overflows, sections are difficult to interact with, and the experience feels like a compressed desktop layout. Since most e-commerce traffic is now mobile, this is costing you sales.

Content & Conversion

There are no trust signals or social proof above the fold. Visitors see product images with no context about why they should choose Xquisite Smoke. Out-of-stock items appear prominently, and key sections like your blog and wholesale offering are buried.

Scope of Work

Phase 1 — SEO & Mobile Fix

A comprehensive technical overhaul addressing all critical SEO and mobile issues:

  • Fix heading structure across all pages
  • Image SEO: alt text, compression, naming conventions
  • Update and optimize metadata (titles, descriptions)
  • Add structured data markup for e-commerce
  • Fix broken links and mobile responsiveness
  • Performance audit and Core Web Vitals optimization

Deliverables: All fixes implemented on live site. Before/after performance report. Google Search Console resubmission.

Timeline: 5–7 working days from approval.

Phase 2 — Content & Conversion Optimisation

Strategic improvements to homepage content, trust signals, review integration, blog visibility, and wholesale landing page optimization.

Phase 3 — Design & Performance

Full mobile-first homepage redesign, image pipeline optimization, script/plugin audit, and Core Web Vitals tuning for faster loads and better rankings.

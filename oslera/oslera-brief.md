## The Opportunity

The NHS Pension Scheme covers **1.7 million active members** — including 300,000+ doctors. It is one of the most complex defined benefit pension schemes in the world, made worse by:
- 1995, 2008 and 2015 scheme transitions
- McCloud remedy (still being implemented)
- Annual allowance charges hitting high earners
- Tapered annual allowance complexity for senior doctors

Most NHS members don't understand their pension. Most IFAs don't have the tools to analyse it efficiently. **Nobody is doing this well with AI.**

## The Business Model

### Two-sided market

**B2C — Doctors direct**
- Free tier: basic pension summary, retirement estimate
- Paid tier: £49–79/year — full analysis, annual allowance check, McCloud impact, what-if scenarios
- Target: 300,000 NHS doctors + 200,000 nurses/AHPs

**B2B — IFAs**
- White-label tool for IFAs to use with NHS clients
- £99–199/month per firm (SaaS)
- Or £30–50 per report (pay-per-use)
- Target: ~500 UK firms specialising in NHS/medical professionals

**Why B2B first:** IFAs already have the client relationships. Each IFA has 50-200 NHS clients. 50 IFA subscribers × £149/month = **£7,500 MRR from day 1**. Validate with 10 IFA beta users before scaling.

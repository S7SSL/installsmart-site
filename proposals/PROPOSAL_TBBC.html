Executive Summary

The Brand Is Strong. The Website Isn't Ready.

The Black Book Collection has a compelling product concept and distinctive brand voice. The Deck of Life planner speaks to a clear audience -- people who want direction, not just organisation. That positioning is rare and valuable.

But the Shopify store has structural issues preventing visitors from becoming customers. Broken navigation, missing products, empty pages, and weak SEO mean the site can't sell in its current state. Month 1 -- The Black Book Shuffle Wheel

Build the Signature Feature New Build

The Black Book Shuffle is the most distinctive element of your brand. Right now it's a basic icon shuffle -- functional but forgettable. We'll replace it with a fully interactive spinning wheel that brings the concept to life.

This isn't just a visual upgrade. It's a reason to come back to the site every day, share with friends, and engage with the brand on a deeper level. The wheel becomes a retention tool and a conversion driver.

  • Custom animated spinning wheel with all four suits
  • Daily focus messages for each suit (Spades, Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs)
  • Mobile-first design -- works perfectly on every device
  • Embedded directly into the Shopify page
  • Shareable results (social-ready output)
  • Analytics tracking on each spin and result

Month 2 -- Fix the Foundations

Make the Store Work Critical Fix

The store is broken. Products don't show, navigation links lead nowhere, and the SEO is invisible. Month 2 strips it back and rebuilds the essentials so visitors can actually find you, browse, and buy.

  • Fix all broken navigation links and page routing
  • Set up product listings with proper descriptions, pricing, and imagery
  • Correct URL structure so every page lives at the right address
  • Fix the empty H1 tag, page titles, and meta descriptions
  • Add alt text to all images for accessibility and SEO
  • Move the About content from the blog URL to a proper page
  • Full mobile responsiveness check and fixes
Month 3 -- Convert & Grow

Turn Visitors Into Customers Growth

Once the store works and the experience is sharp, Month 3 focuses on conversion. We add the trust signals, payment options, and marketing hooks that turn a browser into a buyer.

  • Add card payment options alongside PayPal
  • Build social proof: testimonials, reviews, trust badges
  • Create a branded contact experience (not the Shopify default)
  • Integrate Google Analytics and conversion tracking
  • Connect email signup to a welcome flow (Klaviyo or Mailchimp)
  • Structured data and Open Graph tags for social sharing
  • Launch readiness check and performance audit

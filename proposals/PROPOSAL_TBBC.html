Month 2 -- Fix the Foundations

Get Everything Working Properly Critical Fix

Right now, if someone clicks "Shop Now" they see an empty page -- and a few of the menu links don't go where they should. Month 2 is all about fixing that. We'll get the products showing, the links working, and make sure Google can actually find you.

  • Fix all broken navigation links and page routing
  • Set up product listings with proper descriptions, pricing, and imagery
  • Correct URL structure so every page lives at the right address
  • Fix the empty H1 tag, page titles, and meta descriptions
  • Add alt text to all images for accessibility and SEO
  • Move the About content from the blog URL to a proper page
  • Full mobile responsiveness check and fixes
Month 3 -- Convert & Grow

Start Making Sales Growth

By this point the site looks great and works properly. Now we make it easy for people to actually buy. Card payments, reviews, email capture, analytics -- all the bits that turn "just looking" into "just purchased."

  • Add card payment options alongside PayPal

  • Create a branded contact experience (not the Shopify default)
  • Integrate Google Analytics and conversion tracking
  • Connect email signup to a welcome flow (Klaviyo or Mailchimp)
  • Structured data and Open Graph tags for social sharing
  • Launch readiness check and performance audit

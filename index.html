// Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is "independent" about this advisory?+

Sat has no software to sell, no tech partnerships that pay referral fees, and no incentive to recommend any particular vendor or platform. Every recommendation is made purely on the basis of what's right for your business. Most AI advisors are either vendors themselves or closely affiliated with specific platforms — that conflict shapes every recommendation they make. Independence isn't a marketing claim here, it's the entire value proposition.

We've already approved an AI budget — how do you fit in?+

That's the ideal entry point. You have the mandate and the budget — what you need is confidence that it gets spent on the right things. The Strategy Day is designed exactly for this: arrive with a budget and a general direction, leave with a specific architecture, a prioritised roadmap, and a clear understanding of what to build, what to buy, and who to hire. The Advisory Retainer then keeps Sat involved as the investment is deployed.

Do you do the actual building and installation?+

No — and that's deliberate. The advisor and the implementer should be different people; combining them creates the vendor conflict we're here to remove. What Sat does is define what gets built, recommend who should build it, and oversee the delivery. In straightforward cases where a simple internal setup is the right answer, guidance on that can be part of the strategy output — but the advisory model doesn't extend to hands-on build work.

What size of business is this for?+

London SMEs, typically with 10–200 employees, where a Director or above is driving the AI agenda. The sweet spot is a business that has approved an AI budget and has a clear mandate to act — but needs someone to ensure that investment is deployed intelligently. Enterprise AI playbooks don't translate to this context, and neither do one-size-fits-all tools. That's exactly the gap this advisory fills.

What do I walk away with after a Strategy Day?+

A written output covering: a clear AI architecture for your business, a prioritised implementation roadmap, a shortlist of recommended vendors and tools with reasoning, an honest assessment of what's worth building versus what to ignore, and an ROI framing you can use for internal stakeholders. Delivered within 48 hours of the session. You can take this to any build team, any vendor, or use it as the basis for a procurement process — with or without further involvement from Sat.

Why "InstallSmart" if you don't do installs?+

Fair question. The brand predates the current advisory focus. The name reflects a core principle that hasn't changed: the smartest businesses don't rush to install AI — they install the right AI. That's the work. The advisory is the evolved form of that original insight.

How do I get started?+

Book the free 15-minute discovery call. That's a genuine conversation — no pitch, no slide deck. Sat will ask about where you are with AI, what's driving the agenda, and what you're trying to solve. If there's a fit, you'll know what the right engagement looks like by the end of that call.

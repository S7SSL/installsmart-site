FAQ

Why does my website need schema markup?

Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity pull information from schema markup to answer user questions. Without it, your site is invisible to AI search.

What if I already have good SEO?

Traditional SEO gets you in Google search results. AI readiness gets you cited directly in AI-generated answers — a completely different channel with growing importance.

How long does implementation take?

Initial schema setup takes 2–4 weeks. Ongoing monthly maintenance is 4–8 hours/month depending on site complexity.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There's no lock-in. You can pause or cancel at any time. Monthly billing.

Do you handle the whole website or just schema?

We handle all the structured content and schema work. You keep control of your site. We work via GitHub or direct file uploads.
